Fraternity, Charity, and Personal Excellence

The Grand Lodge of Nigeria brings together men of good character to promote brotherhood, support charitable causes, and foster personal growth through our time-honored traditions.

Our Distinguished History

Founded in 1914, the Grand Lodge of Nigeria stands as the premier fraternal organization in the nation, with a rich heritage of fostering brotherhood and contributing to societal development.

Our organization was established during the colonial era as a beacon of unity and enlightenment, bringing together men from diverse backgrounds who shared common values of integrity, mutual respect, and service to humanity.

Today, with lodges in all 36 states of the federation and over 50,000 members, we continue to uphold these noble traditions while adapting to the needs of modern Nigeria.

Our members include professionals, entrepreneurs, academics, and leaders from all walks of life who are committed to making a positive difference in their communities.
